# ***Flowlit Community Guidelines***

Flowlit is a friendly place for readers and writers. Please be respectful and kind to everyone.

**Be Nice:**

* Treat others with respect.
* Use kind words.
* Give helpful feedback.

**Don't:**

* Harass or bully anyone.
* Share mean or hateful content.
* Post anything illegal or that violates copyright.
* Share private information.
* Try to break the website.

**If you see something wrong:**

* Tell us at [insert contact email/form].

We might remove content or ban users who break these rules.

We want Flowlit to be safe and fun for everyone!
